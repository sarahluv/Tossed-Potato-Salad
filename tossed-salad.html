TOSSED SALAD

Simple and attractive

1 large bunch of greens, preferably mixed
10 cherry tomatoes quartered

DRESSING
1/2 cup Mayonnaise
2 tbsp. Minced dilled or sweet pickle
1 tsp. Prepared mustard
1/4 tsp. Onion powder
1 tsp. Granulated sugar
1 tbsp. Milk

Have greens ready in separate containers in refrigerator.

Dressing: Combine all ingredients together in a small bowl. Mix well. Spoon over greens. Toss to coat. Add tomato and toss together lightly. Serves 8
